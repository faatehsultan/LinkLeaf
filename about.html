About This Project

Link Leaf is world's first and only open source platform where user can create profile and add all his online presence links at one place. This project is targeted to make the contact sharing hasle free and easy. It's kind of Digital Business Card for your online presence. What's more? Our designers are building large number of templates and designs for you to make your LinkLeaf catchy. It completely free and open source. So developers can also contribute in this project. Only the design department is open-source, the core managagement system is secure and highly encrypted for user security. Stay Tuned for more updates.
